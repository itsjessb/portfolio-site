Cookie Notice

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This page explains what cookies and similar technologies jessicabargenquast.com uses today, and what's planned. A cookie is a small file a site can ask your browser to store to remember information between visits.

Cookies this site sets today

None. jessicabargenquast.com does not set its own cookies. The hidden spam-prevention field on the contact and playbook forms is not a cookie; it's a standard form field that stays invisible to real visitors.

Requests to other servers

Loading this site causes your browser to talk to a couple of outside services, each governed by its own policy rather than a cookie set by this site:

  • Google Fonts. Pages request typeface files directly from Google, which means Google's servers see the requesting device's IP address.
  • Netlify. Submitting the contact or playbook form sends that data to Netlify for processing and hosting.

Analytics cookies (planned, not yet active)

Jessica intends to add Google Analytics to this site to understand traffic and improve it. Once that happens, Google Analytics will set cookies such as _ga and _ga_* in your browser to measure things like pages viewed, time on site, and how you arrived here. This notice will be updated with the specifics, and a consent step will be added if your region requires one, before that tracking goes live.

Affiliate and referral link cookies (planned, not yet active)

Some outbound links may in the future be affiliate or referral links (see the Privacy Policy). Those links may pass a tracking code, or cause the destination site to set a short-lived cookie on its own domain, to credit the referral. That cookie would be set and controlled by the destination site, not by jessicabargenquast.com.

Managing cookies

Most browsers let you view, block, or delete cookies in their settings. Once analytics is added, you'll also be able to opt out of Google Analytics specifically using Google's opt-out browser add-on. Blocking cookies may affect how well any future interactive features on this site work, but the core content will always remain accessible.

Changes to this notice

This notice will be updated whenever what this site actually does changes, most notably when analytics or affiliate tracking goes from planned to active. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent change.
